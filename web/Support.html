Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Some quick solutions to common issues:

  • Issue: SSH Alert not connecting to server
    Solution: Ensure server IP and port are correctly configured.
  • Issue: Notifications not appearing
    Solution: Check notification settings and permissions on your device.
  • Issue: Authentication errors
    Solution: Verify SSH keys or passwords and try again.
